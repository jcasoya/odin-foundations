Lamb Chops

Ingredients:

  • 4 lamb chops
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Steps:

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. In a small bowl, mix together the olive oil, minced garlic, dried rosemary, salt, and black pepper.
  3. Brush both sides of the lamb chops with the olive oil mixture.
  4. Place the lamb chops on the preheated grill and cook for about 4-5 minutes per side for medium-rare, or adjust the cooking time according to your preferred doneness.
  5. Remove the lamb chops from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes before serving.
  6. Serve the lamb chops hot and enjoy!
