Lasagna

Authentic Italian Lasagna

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 jar (24 ounces) pasta sauce
  • 1 can (14 ounces) diced tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps:

  1. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until the meat is browned. Drain any excess fat.
  2. Add the pasta sauce, diced tomatoes, basil, oregano, salt, and black pepper to the skillet. Stir well and simmer for 10 minutes.
  3. Meanwhile, cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  4. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  5. In a 9x13-inch baking dish, spread a thin layer of the meat sauce on the bottom.
  6. Place 3 lasagna noodles on top of the sauce.
  7. Add a layer of the meat sauce over the noodles, followed by a layer of mozzarella cheese.
  8. Repeat the layers of noodles, sauce, and cheese until all the ingredients are used, ending with a layer of cheese on top.
  9. Sprinkle the grated Parmesan cheese evenly over the top.
  10. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  11. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  12. Let the lasagna cool for a few minutes before serving.
